Notes

Warning for 16cm observers: A typical 16cm field will contain a 100 mJy confusing source. In very compact arrays, 300 mJy of confusing flux is common on the shortest spacings. At 16cm, calibrators stronger than 5 Jy are recommended for ATCA arrays shorter than 375m.
